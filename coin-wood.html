Overview

A small arcade idea sharpened for mobile replayability.

Coin Wood is built around fast threat recognition and immediate restart energy. The player dodges falling logs, collects apples, and uses powerups to extend runs without adding friction to the core loop.

The work highlights player control tuning, hazard timing, score pressure, UI clarity, and lightweight progression.
