Nebulo

A calm isometric adventure about jumping across floating platforms, waking fireflies, and exploring a soft atmospheric world.

Discuss the projectAll projects

Overview

Atmosphere, readability, and peaceful puzzle movement.

Nebulo explores a softer side of mobile game design: slow pacing, clean isometric composition, gentle challenge, and progression that feels like discovery instead of pressure.

The project is useful as a portfolio piece because it shows visual direction, level readability, prototype iteration, and scene composition beyond pure arcade mechanics.
