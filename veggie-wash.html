Veggie Wash

A relaxing ASMR cleaning simulator where players wash vegetables, earn coins, upgrade tools, and unlock new produce through satisfying touch-based interactions.

View on Google PlayAll projects

Overview

Designed around satisfying cleaning feedback.

The project focuses on a simple, understandable loop: dirty item, clean interaction, reward, upgrade, and unlock. The goal was to make each touch feel useful while keeping the session calm and mobile-friendly.

It demonstrates end-to-end solo production: Unity gameplay, progression, mobile UI, store release, screenshots, and monetization-ready structure.
