Scrap Race

A truck collection simulator about driving routes, gathering scrap waste, recycling for cash, completing missions, and upgrading a cleanup vehicle.

Ask about testingAll projects

Overview

Mission-based collection with simulator and tycoon pressure.

Scrap Race combines vehicle movement, resource collection, and upgrade goals. The player reads the route, collects waste, converts it into cash, and uses rewards to improve the truck and complete better missions.

The project highlights systems thinking across driving feel, mission structure, economy, visual readability, and testing preparation.
